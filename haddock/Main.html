Builds a n-gram based on an input file and a position. Its length is based on the maximum length of n-grams in the model. When encountering punctuation, the n-gram stops regardless of its length.

Converts a .arpa file into a map of the form [elements of (n-1)-gram] [["probability", "n-th element", "bow"]], i.e. one key holds all possible predicions with their probabilites as value.
